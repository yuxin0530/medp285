About This Project

This project explores the moments I've experienced and transforms them into photographs that never fade away. Each image captures a significant memory, preserving the essence of those experiences for eternity, allowing me to reflect on and cherish them long after they've passed. Through this lens, I aim to convey the beauty of fleeting moments and the power of photography to immortalize our personal histories.
