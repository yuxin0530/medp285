Artist Statement

As a media studies student, my work and creative practice are deeply rooted in an exploration of how media shapes our perceptions and influences our lives. My academic journey has been a quest to understand the intricate relationship between media, and technology, and to harness this understanding in innovative and meaningful ways.

By studying the evolution of the web and mastering current technologies like HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, I aim to harness these tools to create compelling, media-rich websites that are both visually engaging and functionally adaptive. My projects are designed to respond seamlessly to diverse devices and screen sizes, demonstrating a commitment to inclusive and accessible design.
